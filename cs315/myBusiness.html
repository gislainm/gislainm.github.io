MyFin


MyFin is every student's friend when it comes to financial literacy and customized
student loan payment plans that minimize interest paid on those loans. There is an
overwhelming number of american college grads paying hefty interest on their student loans.
What's more troubling is that most students sign up for these loans with no sophisticated
knowledge of how loans and interest on those loans work.


There is multiple companies out there doing similar to what MyFin intends to do
but most of those companies charges users for their services. Some of these companies
are care4yourfuture and salliemae.
MyFin Intends to be a free of charge service available to all students, and it will help
understand they student loans and assist them in creating a feasible payment plan that is
less burdensome and saves them money in the long run by minimizing the interest they have
to pay on their loans.
